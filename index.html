About Us

More Than Contractors.
We're Partners.

QuantroServe (Pty) Ltd is a premium, multi-disciplinary construction and infrastructure solutions company based in South Africa. We integrate technical expertise with disciplined project management to deliver on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

We operate as a full-service contractor, supporting both standalone projects and integrated multi-service contracts — giving clients one accountable partner across multiple disciplines.
